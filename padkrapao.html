Pad Kra Pao (Stir Fried Holy Basil)

Pad Kra Pao Gai/Moo Khai Dao is the perfect dish for Muay Thai fighters.

It is made with rice, minced chicken meat, holy basil and chili amongst others.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Holy basil
  • Chicken (gai)
  • Pork (moo)
  • Fried egg (sunny side up)
  • MSG
  • Red chilli pepper
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Oil
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Thin/light soy sauce

Steps

  1. In a wok over medium high heat, add the oil, shallots and garlic, and fry for 3 minutes.
  2. Add the chilies and cook for another minute. Crank up the heat to high, and add the ground pork, breaking it up into small bits and allowing it to crisp up.
  3. Add the sugar, fish sauce, thin/light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Stir-fry for another minute and deglaze the pan with the broth or water.
  4. Because your pan is over high heat, the liquid should cook off very quickly.
  5. Add the holy basil leaves, and stir-fry until wilted.
  6. Serve this Pork Pad Kra Pao(Holy Basil Stir-fry) over rice!

Som Tum(Raw Papaya Salad)

Som Tum is a laotian dish widespread in Thai esp. Isaan cuisine.

It is made with papaya, soy sprouts, fermented fish sauce and others.

Ingredients

  • Raw green papaya
  • Lemon juice
  • Peanuts
  • Coriander
  • Chili
  • Red chillies

Steps

  1. Add some grated papaya to a bowl.
  2. Add a tablespoon of lemon juice. Then add some fried peanuts to the mixture.
  3. Add coriander, red chillies and chilli sauce
  4. Mix all the ingredients together and serve chilled.
